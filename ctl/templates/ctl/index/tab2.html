Getting us to change the locks is easy using our online booking system!


How it works:

  1. Enter your postcode
  2. Choose the locks you want to change (or take a photo so we know which locks to bring!)
  3. Choose when & where to change the locks
  4. Pay a small deposit (balance on site when the job is done)
  5. We come out to you, change the locks & give you your new shiny keys - simple!
